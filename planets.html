The Star Wars universe is home to a vast array of planets, each with its own unique features and characteristics. From the desert world of Tatooine to the forest moon of Endor, the planets in Star Wars offer a diverse range of environments that serve as backdrops for the series' iconic battles and adventures. Some of the most notable planets in the franchise include Coruscant, a bustling metropolis and the capital of the Galactic Republic; Hoth, an icy planet used as a Rebel Alliance base in The Empire Strikes Back; and Dagobah, a swampy world where Luke Skywalker trained with Jedi Master Yoda. The planets in Star Wars often have their own distinct cultures and civilizations, adding depth and richness to the galaxy far, far away.
